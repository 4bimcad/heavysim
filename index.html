About

We are a team of specialists in the fields of mining and education.

Project Description

Operating heavy machinery requires expensive and complex training, which few can afford. For many, learning on real equipment is inaccessible due to high fuel costs, safety risks and environmental pollution. Our solution, a heavy machinery operation simulator, makes this training accessible at a much lower cost, providing the opportunity to gain experience in a safe virtual environment. Through our simulator, we eliminate the risks of accidents and environmental impact. In addition, advanced mobile network technology, such as 5G, allows operators to work remotely, facilitating the operation of machinery anywhere in the world, promoting global competitiveness and democratizing access to this profession.

Our service offers the following:

  • Realistic control joysticks: Control devices very similar to those used in real heavy machinery, providing an authentic experience.
  • Large, detailed displays: High-resolution monitors display detailed graphics, allowing clear viewing of simulation environments.
  • Advanced PCs with powerful graphics cards: Latest generation computers designed to run simulations with high performance and without interruptions.
  • Ergonomic and comfortable seats: Ergonomically designed chairs that provide comfort during long training sessions.
  • Modern simulation software: Advanced simulation technology accurately replicates heavy machinery operations in a safe, controlled environment.

Our service provides a complete and realistic training experience, with state-of-the-art equipment that facilitates effective and safe training. With our simulators, operators can practice and improve their skills without the risks and high costs of real machinery, preparing them to face the challenges of the sector in a professional and competitive manner.

Benefits

Our benefits are as follows:

Affordable Prices
